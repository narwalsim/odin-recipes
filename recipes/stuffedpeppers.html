Stuffed Peppers Vegetarian

Vegetarian stuffed peppers are tender and slightly sweet, filled with savory grains, beans, and veggies, creating a warm, hearty flavor with a hint of smokiness.

  • 1 tbs Salt
  • 4 Large Green Bell Peppers
  • Olive Oil
  • Chopped Onion
  • Cooked Rice
  • Black beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Salt
  • Mexican Cheese
